What is MWPL?

The Mountain West Paintball League is based on the old school 5 & 10 man paintball. It consists of limited paint and tournament play in the Mountain West. It's comprised of Mountain West states such as Utah, Idaho, and Wyoming.

It's about paintball, not profit.
